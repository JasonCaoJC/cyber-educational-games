Description:

Anti-Phishing Phil is an interactive game that teaches users how to identify phishing URLs, where to look for cues in web browsers, and how to use search engines to find legitimate sites. Users will play as Phil, a young fish trying to find food while avoiding the dangers lurking in InterWeb Bay. Help Phil determine which worms - or URLs - are safe and which are untrustworthy.
